What is a story? What is a music? What is an art?

Part writing, part artwork, part music, and part career fair. *dolphin S.O.N.A.R.*
attempts to explore the cutting edge of the human experience.

Infinity. Singularity. Drake Equation. Chakra. Kinetic NRG.

*dolphin S.O.N.A.R.* is a collection of work by [xxarlando](https://www.instagram.com/_arlandob/). The
stories, music, and art works are inspired by his experiences in a [sensory deprivation tank](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isolation_tank).
